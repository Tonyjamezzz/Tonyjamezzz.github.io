Risk Matrix (most common)
HIGH
Volatility / Drawdowns
  • Price can drop 30–80% in bear markets.
  • Leverage magnifies losses fast.
  • DCA helps reduce timing risk, not market risk.
HIGH
Custody / Platform Risk
  • Exchange hacks, freezes, insolvency.
  • Self-custody: seed loss = irreversible loss.
  • Use reputable platforms + 2FA + consider hardware wallet.
MED
Regulation / Policy
  • Taxes, trading restrictions, KYC/AML shifts.
  • Rules vary by country and can change.
MED
Market Structure
  • Liquidity shocks, large liquidations.
  • Correlation changes (sometimes trades like “risk-on”).
